Find Nearby Food Stalls in 2 Easy Steps!

  • Enable location access on your phone.

    Enable location access for the app by toggling the switch to "On" or selecting "Allow."

  • Explore nearby food stalls.

    Once your current location is displayed on the map, zoom in or browse around to see nearby areas. Look for icons or markers that represent food stalls, restaurants, or eateries. Tap on these icons or markers to view more information about the food stalls. Choose a food stall that interests you and navigate to it using the directions provided by the app if needed.
